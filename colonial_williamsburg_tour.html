Full Historic District Tour: Begin at the Capitol Building and walk down Duke of Gloucester Street toward the College of William & Mary. Visit the Courthouse and Stocks, then explore the Magazine & Guardhouse. Continue to Bruton Parish Church and the nearby Apothecary Shop.
